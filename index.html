About The Lazy Blue Conf

Time can't be beaten except time travellers. In order to be sync with it, manage schedules and make the time flexible. The Lazy Site will be your guide to control the tight schedules and have fun. Even though most can't predict what will happen to be exact, it is better to be prepared.

Expert Speakers

Our Expert speakers unites that being lazy is not wrong. In fact, it is the inspiration of most of the innovation in the world.
